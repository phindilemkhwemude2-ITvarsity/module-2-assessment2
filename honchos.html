HONCHOS



Our story begins in 2005 when we took over an existing store on Langalibalele Street in the heart of Pietermaritzburg in KwaZulu-Natal's bustling capital. With our potent combination of great tasting flame-grilled chicken and passionate service, Honchos quickly became a Pietermaritzburg favourite.

Now, Honchos has more than 70 stores nationwide with plans to grow the brand across the nation. The strong following of Honchos patrons stems not only from our great flavour but from our huge meal portions and the fact that we pride ourselves on providing exceptional quality meals with incredible value.
